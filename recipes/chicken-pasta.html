Chicken Alfredo Pasta

A creamy pasta dish with grilled chicken, Parmesan cheese, and a rich Alfredo sauce.

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces fettuccine pasta
  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, grilled and sliced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped, for garnish

Steps

  1. Cook fettuccine pasta according to package directions; drain.
  2. In a large skillet, heat butter and heavy cream over medium heat until butter is melted.
  3. Add grated Parmesan cheese, stirring constantly until sauce is smooth and heated through.
  4. Toss cooked pasta in Alfredo sauce until well coated.
  5. Serve topped with sliced grilled chicken, season with salt and pepper, and garnish with chopped parsley.
